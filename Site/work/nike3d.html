Here is an eShop concept in which products are viewable in 3D to increase the user experience. We'd like to give the possibility to brands or stores to launch a product in 3D with our eShop. Here is an example with Nike.

With our Technical Partner Bordeaux école numérique, we will be able to make 3D scan of the products, edit it with Blender and import the 3D object in the client browser with three.js.

We also developed a Back-office with Ruby on Rails to upload 3d files and all the product's caracteristics.

Don't hesitate to contact us if you're interested in the project.
